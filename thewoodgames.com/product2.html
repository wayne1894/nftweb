遊戲簡介：
1895年2月24日的破曉時分，雷聲轟隆作響驚醒倫敦。一股濃煙直竄天際預示了災難的來臨：國會大廈裡有一顆炸彈爆炸。維安部隊立刻展開行動，並在爆炸攻擊發生的地點附近逮捕了與叛亂團體有關的一名年輕工人─麥可‧查普曼。

邁克羅夫特·福爾摩斯受命負責調查這起皇室爆炸案，他需要判斷這起爆炸案究竟是孤狼行動，還是龐大陰謀計畫的一部分，對這位待在第歐根尼俱樂部的安樂椅神探來說，很顯然又是一件再 簡單不過的案件，不過就在此時，來自薩里的農村夫婦為了證明自己的孩子麥可是清白的，於是聘請了偵探協助調查，邁克羅夫特這才赫然發現他們聘請的偵探，就是他的弟弟夏洛克‧福爾摩斯。

倫敦城中兩個絕頂天才的對決就此展開。福爾摩斯兩兄弟必須證明麥可是清白的或是有罪的。究竟查普曼是個不幸誤交損友的年輕人，還是牢房裡叛亂團體的一員呢？

Story:
At dawn on the 24th of February, 1895, the sound of thunder woke up London. A big column of smoke foreshadowed the catastrophe: a bomb had exploded in the Houses of Parliament. The security forces reacted immediately and arrested Michael

Mycroft Holmes is in charge of investigating the explosion for the Crown prosecution. Mycroft needs to determine if the attack is the action of a lone wolf or part of a bigger plot. It appears to be another easy case from his comfortable easy chair at the Diogenes Club, when he discovers that the detective hired by Michael’s parents to prove the young man’s innocence is no less than his brother Sherlock Holmes. Michael’s family are Surrey farmers and believe that Michael is innocent.

The duel between the two most brilliant minds in London begins. The Holmes have to prove if Michael is innocent or guilty. Is Chapman an unfortunate young man that fell in the wrong company or a member of an anarchist cell?
